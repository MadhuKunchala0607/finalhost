Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the benefits of scaffolding insurance?

Scaffolding businesses are prone to risks, and scaffolding companies often face various issues. Scaffolding insurance prepares you for site-based accidents, falls, slips, and injuries, even before you start the project. It covers situations such as worker negligence, unforeseen weather events, property damage, and claims from third parties.

2. What insurances do you need for scaffolding?

Some of the essential insurance types for scaffolders include:

  • Public liability insurance
  • Contract works insurance
  • Income protection insurance
  • Tools, equipment, and stock insurance
  • Professional indemnity insurance

Our scaffold insurance experts will help you through the various insurance options based on your requirements. Whether you are a contractor, a wage worker, or a scaffold business owner, we get you an insurance policy that best fits your scenario.

3. Who is liable for scaffolding?

People involved in the project, such as the scaffolding installer, manufacturer, contractors, and property owners, can be held liable for accidents, damage, or loss of life on-site. Liability may also extend to workers under multiple contractors in case of negligence or accidents.
